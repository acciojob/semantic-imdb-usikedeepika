Summary

"Inception" is a science fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan, which explores the concept of shared dreaming and the ability to implant ideas into someone's subconscious. It follows a skilled thief, Dom Cobb, as he embarks on a mission to perform the perfect "inception."

Actors

The film stars Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb, alongside Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Tom Hardy, Ellen Page, and Ken Watanabe.
